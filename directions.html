Get Directions & Learn About Our Offices

Our practice is located at the Concourse Office Plaza in the heart of Skokie. The office enjoys convenient access to Skokie Boulevard and Interstate 94. Found across the street for the Old Orchard Shopping Center, we also easily accessible via public transportation.

Traveling by Car

To find the best route to our offices, use the map found below in the footer. You may also click the link beneath the map to enter in your address to get turn-by-turn directions.

Public Transportation

Our offices are also easily accessible from several different bus and train lines. We are just minutes away from the Skokie Swift Yellow train line. Several bus routes also stop outside Macy's located across the street at the Old Orchard Shopping Center, including the CTA buses #97, #201, #205, #208, and the Pace buses #215, and #422. If you are unsure on which bus or train line you should be using, we recommend using the RTA Trip Finder.
